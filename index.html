Past to Present

Era of forgotten, old and new. Let's take a look together, shall we?

17th Century

The Western Classical Music period was between 1730 and 1820. The music used formality and emphasis on order and hierarchy, and a style that used clearer divisions between parts, brighter contrasts, tone colors. Even though classical music remained, new era came emerging soon.

Early 19th

The 1920’s introduced pop culture into the American lifestyle and introduced society to things such as the jazz music, movies, nightclubs and rock & roll. People used pop culture as a sense of escape from government atrocities, protests, war and violence.

The turn of 20th

The change and development in musical language that occurred around the turn of the 20th century, challenging and reinterpreting older categories of music, the "ModernMusic". Innovations that led musicians approach different harmonies, rythms, melodies and music in itself.
